# Githaforge Knowledge Base

## About Githaforge

Githaforge is a powerful AI chatbot builder platform that enables businesses to create, customize, and deploy intelligent customer support chatbots in minutes. Our platform uses advanced RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) technology to deliver accurate, context-aware responses based on your own knowledge base.

### What is Githaforge?

Githaforge is a no-code platform that allows you to build intelligent AI chatbots without any technical expertise. Simply upload your documents (PDFs, Word files, text files) or scrape content from URLs, customize your chatbot's appearance, and deploy it on your website with a single line of code.
